Homemade Chai Tea Blend

Ingredients

  • 24 green cardamom pods
  • 1 Tbsp whole black peppercorns
  • 2 tsp whole cloves
  • 4 cinnamon sticks
  • ¼ c candied ginger
  • ½ c loose leaf black tea

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Mix cardamom pods, peppercorns, cloves and cinnamon sticks on a baking tray with sides. Place in the oven and toast for 5 minutes, or until fragrant. Remove from the oven and let cool.
  3. While the spices are cooling, chop the candied ginger.
  4. Using a sharp knife, split the toasted cardamom pods in half so that the seeds are loose. Chop the cinnamon sticks into small pieces.
  5. In a small bowl, mix ginger, tea, and toasted spices. Divide into jars for storing or gifting.
