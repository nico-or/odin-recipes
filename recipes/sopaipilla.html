Sopaipillas

Description

Sopaipillas are a typical Chilean dish. During the winter months you can found street stalls preparing them in every busy intersection. Since they're easy to make they too are a frequent home treat for the once (evening light meals).

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of squash, coocked and ground
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 cups of white flour
  • 1/2 cup of milk or hot water
  • 1 lt of oil for frying (optional)
